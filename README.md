# The-Bloomberg-Terminal-for-African-Carbon
DMRVX is a data marketplace that packages verified Digital Measurement, Reporting & Verification (DMRV) data from African climate projects into subscription APIs and analytics products for global ESG investors.

The Problem We Solve
Institutional investors managing over $35 trillion in ESG-aligned assets need auditable, real-time emissions data to make informed allocation decisions. Yet African climate projects — representing some of the highest-impact carbon reduction opportunities globally — remain severely under-represented in global carbon markets.

The gap isn't a lack of projects. It's a lack of trusted, standardized, machine-readable data. Traditional MRV processes are manual, infrequent, and opaque — making it nearly impossible for quantitative ESG funds to integrate African carbon data into their models.

DMRVX changes this. We aggregate data from IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and on-ground audits into a unified, real-time API — essentially building the Bloomberg Terminal for African Carbon Data.

Our Vision
To become the definitive data infrastructure layer connecting African climate action with global institutional capital.

Transparency & Trust
Every data point is cryptographically hashed and traceable to its source — satellite imagery, IoT sensors, or ground-level audits.

Real-Time Verification
Sub-minute latency data streams give investors the freshest carbon data available anywhere on the planet.

Pan-African Coverage
From Kenyan cookstove programs to Tanzanian mangrove restoration, we aggregate DMRV data across the continent.

FinTech-Grade Analytics
Portfolio risk scanning, verification integrity scores, and price discovery engines purpose-built for ESG fund managers.
